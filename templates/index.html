Blot

Blot is a platform for organizing different types of games involving maps. Currently, the following game modes are in development:

  • Royale: a battle royale style game where players have to visit locations to score points and collect powerups that can help them against their opponents.
  • Route: follow a route of points, but the only information given is your current distance to the next point.
  • Pin: an online quiz where the answer to the questions is a location on a map (inspired by IScout).
